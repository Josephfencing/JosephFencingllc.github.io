About Us

Joseph Fencing LLC is a fencing and deck contractor with over 10 years of hands-on experience. We specialize in fence and gate installation, repair, painting, staining, and weatherproofing, including both manual and automatic gate systems. Our deck services cover design, construction, repair, and full replacement, ensuring durable outdoor spaces built to last. During the winter months, we also provide reliable snow removal services. Joseph Fencing offers free estimates on all projects.
